A round icon button. Used for the nav menu toggle (translucent scrim disc) and footer social links (soft sand disc).

```jsx
<IconButton variant="scrim" size={43} label="Menu" icon={<Icon name="menu" size={16} />} />
<IconButton variant="sand" size={36} label="WhatsApp" icon={<Icon name="whatsapp" size={18} />} href="#" />
```

Variants: `scrim`, `sand`, `ghost`, `solid`. `size` is the diameter in px.
